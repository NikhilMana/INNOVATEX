VISION X
VisionX is an inter-college technical fest
organized by the Department of Computer
Science 
and 
Engineering 
– 
Artificial
Intelligence & Machine Learning, in association
with the Student Club – Innovatex, held at
Maharaja Institute of Technology, Mysore, on
4th of June 2026. It is a celebration of
technology, creativity, and innovation, bringing
together some of the brightest minds to
compete, collaborate, and create. The fest
features four flagship events — Robogenesis,
BrandVerse— each designed to test different
dimensions of technical expertise, problem-
solving, and teamwork. 
VisionX aims to inspire students to push
boundaries, think beyond the ordinary, and
showcase their talents in a dynamic and
engaging environment that blends fun with
learning.
